Looking at the years after 1975, you can see that animation didn't break the top ten grossing movies until "An American Tail" in 1986. Two years afterwards came "Who Framed Roger Rabbit," then the Disney Renaissance took effect the year later with "The Little Mermaid." After then, most of the top-grossing animated movies have been made by Disney and/or Pixar.
